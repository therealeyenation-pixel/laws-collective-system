## PROCUREMENT & APPROVAL PROCESS

Department Managers have discretion over their allocated budgets. All transactions flow through the following approval chain:

### Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approval Chain |
|--------|----------------|
| **Under $1,000** | Manager → Procurement → Finance (CEO receives monthly report) |
| **$1,000 - $5,000** | Manager → Procurement → Finance → CEO approval |
| **Over $5,000** | Full chain + Board notification |

### Approval Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Department Manager** | Identifies need, initiates request, confirms within budget |
| **Procurement (Property Mgmt)** | Verifies vendor, pricing, availability, compliance |
| **Finance** | Confirms budget availability, proper coding, processes payment |
| **CEO** | Final approval (over $1,000), monthly review of all spending |

### Process Flow

1. **Request:** Manager submits purchase request with justification
2. **Procurement Review:** Property Management verifies vendor and pricing
3. **Finance Review:** Finance confirms budget and processes payment
4. **CEO Approval:** Required for purchases $1,000+ (auto-approved below threshold)
5. **Fulfillment:** Property Management procures and provisions
6. **Tracking:** Property Management maintains inventory and license records

### Monthly Reporting

CEO receives monthly spending report including:
- All transactions by department
- Budget utilization percentages
- Upcoming renewals
- Variance analysis

## LICENSE MANAGEMENT POLICIES

**Property Management Responsibilities:**
- Maintain master inventory of all software licenses
- Track license assignments and utilization
- Process renewals before expiration
- Negotiate enterprise pricing
- Conduct quarterly software audits
- Provision/revoke access for onboarding/offboarding

**Security Requirements:**
- All accounts use organizational SSO where available
- Two-factor authentication required
- Password rotation every 90 days
- Immediate access revocation upon separation

**Budget Authority:**
- Department managers manage within allocated budget
- New software over $500/year requires CEO approval
- Annual budget review and adjustment
