## GRANT APPLICATION TIPS

1. **Start with easy wins** - Monthly microgrants build momentum and track record
2. **Stack eligibility** - Apply as women-owned AND minority-owned AND community-focused
3. **Document everything** - Keep EINs, formation docs, mission statements ready
4. **Tell your story** - Generational wealth building is compelling to funders
5. **Track deadlines** - Use the Grant Management module in your system
