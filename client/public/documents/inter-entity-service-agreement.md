## ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### 1.1 Purpose
This Agreement establishes the terms and conditions under which Real-Eye-Nation LLC ("Provider") will provide creative, design, and media production services to The L.A.W.S. Collective, LLC ("Recipient").

### 1.2 Scope of Services
Provider agrees to furnish the following categories of services:

**Design Services:**
- [ ] Brand identity development and maintenance
- [ ] Logo design and variations
- [ ] Marketing collateral design (brochures, flyers, banners)
- [ ] Presentation design (pitch decks, proposals)
- [ ] Social media graphics and templates
- [ ] Website/UI design
- [ ] Print design (business cards, letterhead, signage)

**Media Production Services:**
- [ ] Video production and editing
- [ ] Photography
- [ ] Audio production and podcast editing
- [ ] Animation and motion graphics
- [ ] Documentary production

**Digital Services:**
- [ ] Content creation for digital platforms
- [ ] Email template design
- [ ] Digital advertising creative
- [ ] Interactive media development

## ARTICLE 6: TIMELINE AND PRIORITY

### 6.1 Standard Turnaround
| Project Type | Standard Timeline |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Simple graphics | 2-3 business days |
| Complex design | 5-7 business days |
| Video editing | 7-10 business days |
| Full production | 2-4 weeks |

### 6.2 Rush Services
Rush projects (less than standard timeline) incur:
- **25% surcharge** for 50% time reduction
- **50% surcharge** for 75% time reduction

### 6.3 Priority Levels
- **P1 - Critical:** Same-day response, immediate start
- **P2 - High:** 24-hour response, next available slot
- **P3 - Normal:** Standard queue
- **P4 - Low:** Scheduled for available capacity

## ARTICLE 8: TERM AND TERMINATION

### 8.1 Initial Term
This Agreement shall remain in effect for **_____ year(s)** from the Effective Date.

### 8.2 Renewal
Agreement automatically renews for successive **1-year** terms unless either party provides **60 days** written notice of non-renewal.

### 8.3 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate with **30 days** written notice.

### 8.4 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately upon:
- Material breach not cured within **15 days** of notice
- Insolvency or bankruptcy filing
- Failure to pay invoices within **60 days**

### 8.5 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Provider shall deliver all completed work
- Recipient shall pay for all work completed to date
- Both parties shall return confidential materials
