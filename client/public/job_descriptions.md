## 1. HR Department Lead

### Position Overview

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|

| **Department** | Human Resources |
| **Reports To** | Trust Governance (Shanna Russell) |

| **Location** | Remote with occasional in-person events |

### Position Summary

The HR Department Lead is responsible for building and maintaining the human capital infrastructure of the LuvOnPurpose Family Enterprise. This role oversees all aspects of talent acquisition, onboarding, training coordination, performance management, and employee relations across all five subsidiary entities. The HR Lead serves as the primary point of contact for recruitment and ensures that new team members are properly integrated into the organization's culture and systems.

### Essential Duties and Responsibilities

**Talent Acquisition & Recruitment (30%)**
- Develop and execute recruitment strategies aligned with organizational growth plans
- Create and deliver recruitment pitches to prospective team members
- Screen candidates and coordinate interview processes
- Maintain candidate tracking system and recruitment pipeline
- Partner with Outreach Coordinator to identify talent within community programs

**Onboarding & Integration (25%)**
- Design and manage comprehensive onboarding program for all new hires
- Administer intake forms and create system profiles for new team members
- Coordinate with IT/Platform Administrator for system access setup
- Conduct orientation sessions covering organizational structure, values, and expectations
- Ensure completion of required documentation (agreements, tax forms, etc.)

**Training & Development Coordination (20%)**
- Collaborate with Academy (Cornelius) to assign appropriate training paths
- Track certification progress and completion across all team members
- Identify skill gaps and recommend development opportunities
- Coordinate cross-training initiatives between entities

**Performance Management (15%)**
- Develop and implement performance evaluation frameworks
- Conduct regular check-ins with team members across entities
- Document performance issues and coordinate improvement plans
- Recognize and celebrate achievements and milestones

**Employee Relations & Culture (10%)**
- Serve as first point of contact for workplace concerns
- Mediate conflicts and facilitate resolution
- Plan and coordinate team-building activities
- Maintain employee handbook and policy documentation

### Required Qualifications

- Minimum 3 years experience in human resources, recruiting, or people management
- Demonstrated ability to recruit and retain talent
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills
- Proficiency with HR information systems and applicant tracking
- Understanding of employment law basics (or willingness to learn)

### Preferred Qualifications

- HR certification (PHR, SHRM-CP) or equivalent experience
- Experience in startup or small business environment
- Background in community-based organizations
- Familiarity with remote team management

### Compensation & Benefits

- Ownership stake in The L.A.W.S. Collective, LLC
- All Academy certifications provided at no cost
- Token earnings based on recruitment and retention metrics
- Flexible work schedule
- Voice in Trust governance decisions

## General Employment Information

### Organizational Structure

All positions report ultimately to Trust Governance through Shanna Russell (Founder/Matriarch/Trustee). The Calea Freeman Family Trust serves as the parent organization overseeing five subsidiary entities:

| Entity | Focus Area | State |
|--------|------------|-------|
| LuvOnPurpose Autonomous Wealth System LLC | Services/Consulting | Delaware |
| The L.A.W.S. Collective, LLC | Products/Platform | Delaware |
| Real-Eye-Nation LLC | Media/Content | Georgia |
| LuvOnPurpose Outreach Temple and Academy Society, Inc. | Training/Education | Georgia |
| Calea Freeman Family Trust | Asset Holding | Jamaica |

### Ownership Structure

All positions include an ownership stake in the relevant entity. Ownership percentages are determined based on role, contribution level, and tenure. Ownership provides:

- Voting rights on entity decisions
- Share of entity profits
- Voice in Trust governance
- Long-term wealth building opportunity

### Training Requirements

All team members are required to complete:

1. **Financial Literacy Certification** - Academy course on personal and business finance
2. **Organizational Orientation** - Overview of all entities, structure, and systems
3. **Role-Specific Training** - Certifications relevant to assigned position
4. **Ongoing Development** - Continuous learning through Academy courses

### Performance Expectations

Team members are expected to:

- Complete assigned responsibilities with quality and timeliness
- Collaborate effectively across entities and departments
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
- Represent the organization professionally
- Contribute to organizational culture and values
- Participate in required training and development

### Application Process

1. **Express Interest** - Contact Shanna Russell or current team member
2. **Complete Intake Form** - Personal profile and skills assessment
3. **Interview** - Discussion of role fit and expectations
4. **Academy Onboarding** - Complete required certifications
5. **Shadow Period** - Work alongside current team to learn systems
6. **Formal Assignment** - Receive role, entity stake, and responsibilities
