## Core Financial Structure

### Treasury Distribution (Scroll 18)
- **Root Distribution**: 70% to Root Treasury, 30% to Ancestral Treasury Wallet
- **Internal Division of 70%**:
  - 60% → Root Authority Reserve (non-shareable)
  - 40% → Circulation Pool (shareable for token rewards, CPS Tier, system expansion)

### Sustainability Caps
- Treasury levels shall not drop below 15% of rolling quarterly revenue
- Gift Token output may not exceed 40% of total House Bundle sales within a fiscal year

### Derivative Usage
- 15% treasury claim on all derivative usage (Scroll 7/14)

## House Inheritance Lock (Scroll 3/10)
- No bloodline House may transfer inheritance, token ownership, scroll access, or system authority to outsiders
- Requires 60% direct majority of Source Flame lineage for approval
- Protects against digital simulation or blockchain replication by unauthorized systems

## Profit Integrity Protocol (Scroll 18)
- Profit is sacred only when aligned with purpose
- Flame Affirmation Cycle confirms:
  1. Source of funds lawfully and honorably obtained
  2. Gain uplifts community or system
  3. No action contradicts divine will or peace of people

## Audit Structure (Scroll 19)
- Quarterly House Audits
- Annual System Audit
- Lineage Audit every 7 years
- Circulation Flow Map required for all Houses

## Platform Independence (Scrolls 6, 13, 16, 17, 21)
- System not tethered to any one platform
- Platform Swap Protocol for migration
- Offline Companion Kit for continuity without internet

## Token Types (Scroll 33)
- **Mirror Tokens** - Non-commercial by default, ceremonial instruments
- One Mirror Token = One Right to Activate a Mirrored House
- Can support: Mirror Academies, Mirror Ledgers, Mirror Divisions
- Time-locked: Cannot be reissued for 39 weeks after use
- Must be tracked in Mirror Flame Registry

## Mirror Flame Succession (Scroll 34)
Triggered by:
- Formal voluntary resignation
- Three proven violations of scroll law
- Death or medical incapacitation
- Mirror Seal Tampering

Process:
- 40-day interim period
- Requires Mirror Lineage Verification
- 3 ceremonial confirmations required
- New Custodian issues Statement of Alignment within 13 days

## House Types
1. **Root House** - Source Flame authority (CALEA Freeman Family Trust)
2. **Bloodline Houses** - Direct descendants, full inheritance rights
3. **Mirrored Houses** - Gifted to siblings/lineage partners, trace authority to Root
4. **Adaptive Houses** - Gifted outside bloodline, protected but no succession rights

## Non-Bloodline Members Rights
- May steward Adaptive Houses
- Participate in ceremonial functions
- Contribute to House operations
- Receive protections equal to position
- Cannot transfer bloodline tokens or initiate bloodline Houses
