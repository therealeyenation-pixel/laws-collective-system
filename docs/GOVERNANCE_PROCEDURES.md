# Governance Procedures Manual

## Overview

This manual outlines the governance procedures for The L.A.W.S. Collective and its subsidiary entities. It covers decision-making processes, voting procedures, escalation paths, and conflict resolution mechanisms.

## Governance Structure

### Hierarchy

```
Board of Directors
    │
    ├── Executive Committee
    │       │
    │       ├── CEO / Managing Director
    │       ├── CFO / Finance Director
    │       └── COO / Operations Director
    │
    └── Advisory Committees
            │
            ├── Finance Committee
            ├── Education Committee
            ├── Media Committee
            └── Commercial Committee
```

### Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities | Decision Authority |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| Board | Strategic direction, major decisions | Full |
| Executive | Day-to-day operations | Operational |
| Committee | Advisory, specialized decisions | Domain-specific |
| Member | Participation, voting | Voting rights |

## Decision Types

### Tier 1: Board Decisions

Requires full board approval:
- Entity creation or dissolution
- Major allocation changes (>10%)
- Executive appointments
- Policy changes affecting all entities
- Emergency actions

**Approval Threshold**: 75%
**Voting Period**: 7 days
**Quorum**: 60%

### Tier 2: Executive Decisions

Requires executive committee approval:
- Budget allocations (<$100,000)
- Hiring decisions (non-executive)
- Operational policy changes
- Vendor contracts (<$50,000)

**Approval Threshold**: 67%
**Voting Period**: 3 days
**Quorum**: 50%

### Tier 3: Committee Decisions

Requires relevant committee approval:
- Department budgets
- Project approvals
- Process changes
- Minor policy updates

**Approval Threshold**: 51%
**Voting Period**: 2 days
**Quorum**: 40%

### Tier 4: Member Decisions

Open to all members:
- Community initiatives
- Feature requests
- Event planning
- Recognition awards

**Approval Threshold**: 51%
**Voting Period**: 5 days
**Quorum**: 25%

## Voting Procedures

### Proposal Submission

1. **Draft Proposal**: Create detailed proposal document
2. **Sponsor**: Obtain required sponsors (varies by tier)
3. **Review**: Submit for preliminary review
4. **Publication**: Proposal published to relevant voters
5. **Discussion**: Open discussion period
6. **Voting**: Formal voting period begins

### Proposal Requirements

| Tier | Sponsors Required | Review Period | Discussion Period |
|------|-------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 3 board members | 7 days | 14 days |
| 2 | 2 executives | 3 days | 7 days |
| 3 | 2 committee members | 2 days | 5 days |
| 4 | 5 members | 1 day | 3 days |

### Voting Methods

**Standard Vote**
- Yes / No / Abstain
- Simple majority or threshold as specified

**Ranked Choice**
- For multiple options
- Instant runoff elimination

**Weighted Vote**
- Token-weighted voting
- Stake-weighted voting

### Vote Counting

```
Approval = (Yes Votes) / (Yes Votes + No Votes) × 100%

If Approval >= Threshold AND Total Votes >= Quorum:
    Decision = APPROVED
Else:
    Decision = REJECTED
```

## Escalation Procedures

### Automatic Escalation

Decisions automatically escalate when:
- Voting deadline passes without quorum
- Approval threshold not met but close (within 5%)
- Conflict of interest declared
- Emergency flag raised

### Escalation Path

```
Level 1: Department Manager
    ↓ (24 hours)
Level 2: Division Head
    ↓ (48 hours)
Level 3: Executive Committee
    ↓ (72 hours)
Level 4: Board of Directors
    ↓ (168 hours)
Level 5: External Arbitration
```

### Emergency Escalation

For urgent matters:
1. Flag as emergency
2. Notify all relevant parties
3. Convene emergency session (4 hours)
4. 90% approval required
5. Immediate implementation

## Conflict Resolution

### Types of Conflicts

1. **Resource Conflicts**: Competing resource requests
2. **Policy Conflicts**: Disagreements on policy interpretation
3. **Personal Conflicts**: Interpersonal disputes
4. **Entity Conflicts**: Inter-entity disagreements

### Resolution Process

**Step 1: Direct Resolution**
- Parties attempt direct resolution
- Document discussion and outcomes
- Timeline: 3 days

**Step 2: Mediation**
- Neutral mediator assigned
- Structured mediation session
- Timeline: 7 days

**Step 3: Arbitration**
- Formal arbitration panel
- Binding decision
- Timeline: 14 days

**Step 4: Appeal**
- Appeal to higher authority
- Final decision
- Timeline: 21 days

### Mediator Selection

Mediators are selected based on:
- No conflict of interest
- Relevant expertise
- Availability
- Previous mediation success rate

## Emergency Procedures

### Emergency Declaration

An emergency can be declared by:
- Board chair
- Two executive committee members
- Unanimous committee vote

### Emergency Powers

During emergencies:
- Expedited voting (4 hours)
- Reduced quorum (25%)
- Increased threshold (90%)
- Immediate implementation

### Post-Emergency Review

After emergency resolution:
- Full documentation required
- Board review within 7 days
- Lessons learned documented
- Process improvements identified

## Amendment Procedures

### Governance Document Amendments

1. Propose amendment
2. Legal review
3. Board discussion
4. Member notification (30 days)
5. Board vote (80% required)
6. Implementation

### Policy Updates

1. Draft update
2. Committee review
3. Public comment period
4. Final approval
5. Communication
6. Training (if needed)
