# Salary Recommendations - Market-Competitive Ranges

## Market Research Summary (January 2026)

Based on current market data from BLS, Glassdoor, Salary.com, Indeed, and nonprofit compensation reports, the following salary ranges are recommended to attract and retain highly qualified professionals. **These ranges reflect competitive compensation for degreed professionals with relevant experience.**

---

## Executive Leadership Positions

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO) / Financial Controller
**Market Data:**
- Nonprofit CFO average: $150,000 - $287,000
- Financial Controller: $120,000 - $210,000
- Multi-entity CFO: $175,000 - $250,000

**Recommended Range:** $145,000 - $195,000
**Justification:** For a multi-entity organization requiring sophisticated financial oversight across trusts, LLCs, and 508(c)(1)(a) entities, this range attracts experienced CPAs and financial executives.

## Benefits Package Recommendations

To remain competitive, salary should be supplemented with:

1. **Health Insurance** - Medical, dental, vision (employer pays 80%)
2. **Retirement** - 401(k) or 403(b) with 5% employer match
3. **PTO** - 20 days annually + holidays
4. **Professional Development** - $3,000-$7,500 annual budget
5. **Flexible Work** - Remote/hybrid options where applicable
6. **Performance Bonuses** - 10-20% of base salary for meeting goals
7. **Education Assistance** - Tuition reimbursement up to $5,250/year

## Stipend Policy

Stipends are additional compensation provided to employees who take on duties beyond their primary role. Stipends are separate from base salary and may be adjusted based on workload and organizational needs.

### Eligibility
Any employee may receive stipends for additional cross-entity or specialized duties approved by leadership.

### Stipend Ranges

| Category | Range | Examples |
|----------|-------|----------|
| **Minor Additional Duties** | $3,000 - $6,000 | Committee participation, special projects, mentoring |
| **Moderate Cross-Entity Work** | $6,000 - $12,000 | Pillar leadership, liaison roles, advisory functions |
| **Significant Additional Responsibilities** | $12,000 - $18,000 | Department oversight, specialized expertise, executive advisory |
