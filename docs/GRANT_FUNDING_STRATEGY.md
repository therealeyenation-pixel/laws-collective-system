# BizFlow Pro - Grant Funding Strategy

## Executive Summary

Yes, grant money can fund the development and launch of BizFlow Pro. Multiple grant programs specifically target Black-owned tech businesses, software development, and small business tools. This document outlines a strategic approach to securing $50,000-$1,000,000+ in non-dilutive funding.

The key insight: **Position BizFlow Pro as a tool that helps other small businesses transition from workforce employment to self-employment** — this aligns with your L.A.W.S. Collective mission and resonates with grant funders focused on economic empowerment.

#### Black Ambition Prize [1]

Founded by Pharrell Williams, this is the premier grant for Black tech entrepreneurs.

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Black or Latinx founder(s) with majority ownership
- Scaling ventures in technology, healthcare, consumer products, media, or AI
- Raised less than $1M in dilutive funding
- All team members 18+ years old

**Award Tiers**:
- Grand Prize: $1,000,000
- Runner-up prizes: $250,000, $100,000, $50,000

**Why BizFlow Pro Qualifies**:
- Technology product (SaaS)
- Serves underserved market (micro-businesses)
- Economic empowerment mission
- No prior VC funding

**Application Strategy**:
- Emphasize workforce-to-self-employment transition
- Highlight how tool helps other Black entrepreneurs
- Show traction with L.A.W.S. Collective as proof of concept

#### Google Black Founders Fund [2]

Google provides non-dilutive funding plus Google Cloud credits and mentorship.

**What You Get**:
- $100,000+ cash grant
- $200,000 in Google Cloud credits
- Access to Google mentors and network
- Technical support

**Eligibility**:
- Black-led startup
- Technology-focused
- Demonstrated traction

**Application Strategy**:
- Position as B2B SaaS for small businesses
- Emphasize scalability and market size
- Show technical sophistication of platform

## Federal Grant Programs

### SBIR/STTR Programs

**Current Status**: Congressional authorization expired September 30, 2025. Programs are paused but expected to be reauthorized [5].

**When Available**:
- Phase I: $50,000 - $275,000 (6-12 months)
- Phase II: $750,000 - $1,500,000 (2 years)
- Phase III: Commercialization (no funding limit)

**Relevant Agencies**:
- NSF (National Science Foundation) - Technology innovation
- SBA (Small Business Administration) - Business tools
- DOL (Department of Labor) - Workforce development tools

**Positioning for SBIR**:
- Frame as "workforce development technology"
- Emphasize helping small businesses manage employees
- Show innovation in payroll/HR automation

### EDA (Economic Development Administration) [6]

EDA provides grants for economic development projects.

**Relevant Programs**:
- Regional Technology and Innovation Hubs
- Economic Adjustment Assistance
- Build to Scale Program

**Positioning**:
- Tool that supports small business growth
- Creates jobs by helping businesses scale
- Economic empowerment for underserved communities

## Positioning Strategy

### Core Narrative

> "BizFlow Pro empowers micro-businesses to transition from workforce employment to successful self-employment by providing enterprise-grade business management tools at prices they can afford. Built by a Black-owned family enterprise, our mission is to close the wealth gap by giving every small business owner the same operational capabilities that large corporations enjoy."

### Key Messages by Audience

| Audience | Message |
|----------|---------|
| **Black Entrepreneur Grants** | Tool built by Black founders to help other Black entrepreneurs succeed |
| **Tech/AI Grants** | Innovative SaaS platform using AI to automate business operations |
| **Economic Development** | Creates jobs by helping small businesses scale and hire |
| **Workforce Development** | Enables transition from employee to business owner |

### Proof Points to Develop

1. **L.A.W.S. Collective Case Study**: Show the system working in production
2. **User Testimonials**: Get quotes from family members using the system
3. **Market Research**: Document the 5.3M micro-businesses in the US
4. **Technical Innovation**: Highlight unique features (offline-first, auto-sync)
