# House/Trust Architecture

## Overview

The House/Trust system is the foundational structure of the LuvOnPurpose ecosystem. A "House" represents a family unit's business/trust structure that participates in the collective wealth-building system. Houses can exist in two states:

1. **Template/Placeholder** - Structure defined but no legal entity formed
2. **Activated** - Legal entity formed and verified, full system access

## The 98 Trust - Root Structure

The 98 Trust is the apex entity that governs the entire ecosystem:
- Holds IP rights to LuvLedger
- Receives royalties from L.A.W.S. Business OS licensing
- Distributes to member Houses based on participation
- Provides governance framework for all Houses

## House Hierarchy

```
The 98 Trust (Root)
├── Founding House (Your Family) - MUST ACTIVATE FIRST

│   └── Family Trust Structure
│
├── Template Houses (Placeholders)
│   ├── House Template A (Employee transitions here)
│   ├── House Template B (External partner structure)
│   └── House Template C (Contractor network member)
│
└── Activated Houses (Real entities)
    ├── [Employee Name] Family Trust
    ├── [Partner Name] Holdings, LLC
    └── [Contractor Name] Enterprises
```

## House States

### 1. TEMPLATE (Placeholder)
- Structure exists in system
- No legal entity
- Cannot receive distributions
- Can view educational content
- Can track progress toward activation

### 2. PENDING_ACTIVATION
- User has initiated activation
- Entity formation in progress
- Documents being collected
- Verification pending

### 3. ACTIVATED
- Legal entity verified (LLC, Corp, or Trust)
- EIN confirmed
- Bank account connected
- Operating agreement on file
- Can receive distributions
- Full system access

### 4. SUSPENDED
- Previously activated but compliance issue
- Distributions held
- Must resolve to reactivate

## Activation Requirements

### For Employee-to-Contractor Transition Houses
1. Complete 24-month employment tenure
2. Complete all 8 transition training modules
3. Form legal entity (LLC recommended)
4. Obtain EIN
5. Open business bank account
6. Sign contractor agreement with L.A.W.S. Collective
7. Pay first month platform subscription
8. House activation ceremony (optional but encouraged)

### For External Partner Houses
1. Application and vetting
2. Form legal entity
3. Sign partnership agreement
4. Initial capital contribution or commitment
5. House activation

### For Business-First Houses (Existing Businesses)
1. Business verification (proof of existing, functioning business)
2. Complete all 8 simulator training modules (REQUIRED)
3. Sign ecosystem participation agreement
4. Choose voluntary contribution rate (0-100%, owner's choice)
5. House activation

**CRITICAL: Business-First Revenue Model**
- The existing business revenue does NOT automatically flow into the House
- The business remains 100% independent and owner-controlled
- Owner receives distributions from the COLLECTIVE pool (not their own business)
- Owner may VOLUNTARILY contribute a % of their business revenue
- Contribution is optional and owner-determined
- Distribution eligibility requires training completion regardless of business success

**How the System Benefits from Business-First Houses:**

The 60/40 and 70/30 splits apply to ALL revenue flowing through platform tools:

| Revenue Stream | Description | Split Applied |
|---------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Platform Subscription** | Monthly access fee for L.A.W.S. Business OS | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **Tool Usage Fees** | Per-transaction fees (payroll, invoicing, contracts, tax prep) | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **Training/Certification** | Required course fees | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **Referral Commissions** | Bringing new businesses to ecosystem | 70/30 Intra-House |
| **Marketplace Fees** | Selling through L.A.W.S. marketplace | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **API Access Tiers** | Premium integrations | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **White-Label Services** | Using infrastructure under their brand | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **Insurance Pool** | Collective insurance participation | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **Asset Access Fees** | Using collective property resources | 60/40 Inter-House |
| **Voluntary Contributions** | Optional revenue sharing | Owner's choice |

**Key Principle**: Their business stays independent, but every interaction with ecosystem tools generates value for the collective.

## Trust Structure Within Houses

Each activated House can have internal trust structures:

```
[Family Name] House
├── Operating Entity (LLC/Corp)
│   └── Business operations, contracts, income
├── Family Trust
│   ├── Asset protection
│   ├── Estate planning
│   └── Generational transfer
├── Education Trust (optional)
│   └── Children's education funding
└── Charitable Trust (optional)
    └── Giving and community support
```

## Inheritance & Succession

### House Succession Rules
1. Primary successor designated by House founder
2. If no designation, eldest adult child
3. If no children, spouse/partner
4. If no family, assets return to The 98 Trust
5. Succession requires 30-day notice and acceptance

### Distribution Inheritance
- Active distributions continue to successor
- Historical distributions remain with original recipient
- Pending distributions transfer with House

## Token Integration

Houses interact with the token system:
- **MIRROR Token** - Identity verification, House registration
- **GIFT Token** - Contributions to other Houses
- **SPARK Token** - Activation trigger
- **HOUSE Token** - Represents activated House status
- **CROWN Token** - Governance rights in The 98 Trust

## Placeholder House Benefits

Even before activation, placeholder Houses can:
1. Access educational content
2. Track activation progress
3. View (not receive) potential distributions
4. Participate in community events
5. Build relationships with other Houses
6. Accumulate "activation credits" through participation

## Distribution Tiers

Houses progress through tiers based on participation, NOT business revenue:

| Tier | Requirements | Distribution Share | Benefits |
|------|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Observer** | Registered, training not complete | 0% | Educational access, community events |
| **Participant** | Training complete, active 6+ months | Base share | Standard distributions, voting rights |
| **Contributor** | Voluntary contributions made | 1.5x base | Enhanced distributions, priority access |
| **Partner** | 24+ months active, significant contribution | 2x base | Full partner benefits, governance role |

**Key Principle**: Distribution eligibility is based on ecosystem participation and training completion, NOT on the success or revenue of an external business.

```
L.A.W.S. Business OS Revenue

```
