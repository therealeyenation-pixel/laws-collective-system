### Why This Structure Is Legally Sound

1. **The 70/30 Split** is a legitimate "Platform Services Fee" or "Administrative Services Agreement" - this is how holding companies charge subsidiaries for shared services (accounting, legal, HR, etc.).

2. **The 60/40 House Internal Split** separates:
   - **Reserve (60%)**: Non-shareable operating capital - standard for any business
   - **Community Share (40%)**: Heir distributions - mirrors trust beneficiary distributions

3. **Heir Distribution with Vesting** is exactly how spendthrift trusts work:
   - Age-based milestones (18, 21, 25)
   - Education requirements
   - Participation requirements
   - Protection from creditors

4. **Community Fund Allocations** mirror charitable remainder trusts and family foundation structures.
